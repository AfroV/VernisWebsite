Your NFTs Aren't Truly Safe

Owning the token isn't the same as owning the art. Here's why collectors need Vernis.

Centralized Gateways Fail

Most NFT platforms rely on centralized IPFS gateways. When they go down, your art disappears — even though you still own the token.

No Local Backup

Your NFT metadata points to files hosted elsewhere. Without a local copy, you're trusting third parties with your collection's existence.

Hidden Single Points of Failure

Even "decentralized" storage can have single points of failure. True ownership means having the files in your possession.

Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if Vernis the company disappears?
Nothing. Your Vernis device runs completely independently. It's a standard Raspberry Pi running open-source software. You own the hardware, the files are stored locally, and you can maintain it yourself indefinitely.
How does the NFT redemption work?
After minting, you can burn your NFT at any time to claim the physical device. Burning triggers our fulfillment process — we'll collect your shipping details and send your Vernis unit. The NFT can also be traded before burning.
What NFTs does Vernis support?
Vernis supports Ethereum-based NFTs including images, GIFs, and video. Connect your wallet, and Vernis automatically fetches and backs up all compatible artwork to local IPFS storage.
Can I display art from multiple wallets?
Yes. You can connect multiple wallets to your Vernis device and curate a unified gallery from all your collections through the web-based management interface.
What's the difference between Premium and Standard?
Premium features a powerful Raspberry Pi 5 with a stunning 2K OLED display in a handcrafted wooden enclosure — designed as a statement piece. Standard uses a Pi Zero 2W with a compact 4" touch display — perfect for desktop display and backup functionality.
Do I need technical knowledge to set it up?
No. Vernis arrives pre-configured. Simply connect to power and Wi-Fi, then access the web interface from any device on your network to connect your wallet and start preserving.
